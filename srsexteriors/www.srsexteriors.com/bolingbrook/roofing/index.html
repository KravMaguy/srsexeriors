Without a doubt, your roof is one of the most expensive elements of your home. That’s why doing routine inspections and maintenance is so important. At SRS Exteriors, we provide a wide selection of roofing services, ranging from inspections to replacements, all for affordable rates. 

As a premier roofing contractor in the Bolingbrook area, we provide comprehensive residential and commercial roofing solutions for homes and businesses of all sizes. Whether you need a completely new roof installed or just need some small repairs to maintain your current roof’s integrity, we make it our job to protect you from the elements. 

We offer the following services: 

  • Roofing Replacement and Repair
  • Commercial Roofing Solutions 
  • Attractive Roof Designs 

Expert Roofing Repairs and Replacements 

Damage to your roof is an expensive problem on its own, but it can also lead to a host of other issues. At SRS Exteriors, we specialize in taking care of your roof. Whether that means doing preemptive work to protect it or repairing it once something breaks, we provide comprehensive Bolingbrook roofing services. 

Our process usually starts with an inspection, where we comb over your roof to find any problems or issues that could arise in the future. Once we diagnose what issues are there, we can complete any repairs that are needed with our complete arsenal of roofing options and materials. 

When your entire roof begins aging and deteriorating, we can complete an affordable roof replacement that will last for decades to come.

Diverse Selection of Stylish Roofing Options 

At SRS Exteriors, we understand that your roof isn’t just there to protect your home from the elements; it’s also there to improve the visual appeal of your home. That’s why we offer different roofing options with different looks, each of which are backed by lifetime warranties. These are two of our most popular roof types:
