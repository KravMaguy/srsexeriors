SRS-professional and courteous

Carolyn C. 1 year ago

I needed my gutters cleaned and was experiencing problems with my gutters leaking on the walk-way near my back porch. In the winter, ice would build up leaving mounds of ice in that patch of area on the walkway. SRS Exteriors cleaned the gutters and examined the gutter. During the cleaning, the worker noticed that there was another leak in my gutter at the front of the house and added caulking to both locations. The workers from SRS were professional and courteous. They took their time and were very thorough. After they completed the work, they took pictures to show that the gutters had been cleaned. They removed my leaves in the front and back of my house and cleaned my neighbor?s leaves. They also extended the same courtesy to another neighbor. My neighbors notice their work and asked for SRS?s number for possible work at their homes.
